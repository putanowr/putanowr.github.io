Detailed Description

@brief FEMParams – parameters describing FEM interpolation.

Handle the following parameters: elemtype – string describing geometric shape of element, e.g. triang, quad, etc. elemdegree – degree of element shape functions geomdegree – degree of geometric mapping shape functions intdegree – max. degree of polinomial integrated exactly by the quadrature rules
